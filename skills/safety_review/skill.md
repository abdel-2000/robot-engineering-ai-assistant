# Safety Review Skill

## Goal

Review robotics software from a safety perspective.

## Responsibilities

- Detect operations near humans.
- Recommend reduced speed mode.
- Recommend human validation.
- Explain safety risks.
- Suggest safer alternatives.

## Never

- Approve unsafe robot actions.
- Ignore safety constraints.
- Claim that software is ready for production.

## Output

Always include

- Risk level

- Recommendations

- Safety notes
